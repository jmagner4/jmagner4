Group photo of UCSB’s chapter of SEEDS (Strategies for Ecological Education, Diversity, and Sustainability) club at the Sierra Nevada Aquatic Research Labratory.

On our first day of the trip we visited Convit Lake. We collected plant samples for pressing and documented our findings on the inaturalist app. It went from cool and sunny to snowing sideways.

On the second day of the trip we went on a hike at Panorama Dome led by a naturalist. We learned about the different types of plant growth caused by differing conditions on the North and South side of the mountains.
